Overview

Stay in the charming Paris Boulogne area

Located in the western business district of Paris, 3.5 km from the Eiffel Tower, the Radisson Blu stands in the Boulogne-Billancourt area between the two congress centers in Porte Maillot and Porte de Versailles. Top attractions like the Roland Garros and Parc des Princes stadiums are just minutes away, and nearby bus, metro, and tramway lines allow you to easily traverse the city and explore all its main attractions.
